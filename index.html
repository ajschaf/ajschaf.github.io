Aaron Schaffer

I'm a researcher working on the Cybersecurity 202 and Technology 202 newsletters at The Washington Post. I previously studied investigative journalism as a student in American University's Journalism and Public Affairs master's program.

I previously worked at Al-Monitor, a news organization covering the Middle East and North Africa, from 2016 to 2020. In 2020, I received a Best in Business Award from the Society of American Business Editors and Writers (SABEW) for our newsletter on Middle East lobbying.

You can contact me via LinkedIn, Twitter, or email (aaron at aaronschaffer dot com).
