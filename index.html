Aaron Schaffer is an assistant editor working on the Cybersecurity 202 and Early 202 newsletters at The Washington Post. Before becoming an assistant editor, he was a researcher for the Cybersecurity 202 and Technology 202 newsletters. He previously studied investigative journalism as a student in American University's Journalism and Public Affairs master's program.

Aaron used to work at Al-Monitor, a news organization covering the Middle East and North Africa, from 2016 to 2020. In 2020, he received a Best in Business Award from the Society of American Business Editors and Writers (SABEW) for a newsletter on Middle East lobbying.

You can contact Aaron via LinkedIn or email (aaron at aaronschaffer dot com). You can also follow him on Mastodon or Twitter.
