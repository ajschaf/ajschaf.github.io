Aaron Schaffer

I'm a journalist who loves covering foreign lobbying and influence in Washington.

I'm currently studying investigative journalism as a student in American University's Journalism and Public Affairs master's program.

I worked at Al-Monitor, a news organization covering the Middle East and North Africa, from 2016 to 2020. In 2020, I received a Best in Business Award from the Society of American Business Editors and Writers (SABEW) for our newsletter on Middle East lobbying.

You can contact me via LinkedIn, Twitter, or email (aaron at aaronschaffer dot com).
